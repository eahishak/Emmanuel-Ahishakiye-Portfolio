Hi,
I am Emmanuel Ahishakiye, simply call me Emmanuel!

Emmanuel is a dedicated student at the University of Rochester, pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Business with a minor in Digital Media Studies. His academic journey, combined with practical experience, has equipped him with a diverse skill set encompassing web development, artificial intelligence, project management, and effective communication. Throughout his academic and professional career, he had the privilege of collaborating with both start-ups and nonprofit organizations. Currently, he is engaged in a remote part-time position as a Founder, Spokesperson, and CTO at Jackal Tech Ltd, a dynamic tech startup. In this capacity, he is spearheading the design and development of an innovative e-commerce web application. Emmanuel is not one to rest on his laurels; he is consistently on the lookout for new opportunities that contribute to both his personal and professional growth. His proactive approach to seeking challenges and learning experiences underscores his ambition to make a meaningful impact in the dynamic world of technology.
